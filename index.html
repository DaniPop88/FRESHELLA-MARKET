About Us

Locally Grown, Family-Owned, Community-Focused

Freshella Market Place started in 2021 as a small home retail shop with a mission to make affordable, high-quality household goods accessible to families in Palawan.
Inspired by the simplicity and warmth of Filipino homes, Freshella grew into a full-service market known for its wide selection of trusted brands and local finds. We proudly support local suppliers and maintain strong ties with barangay communities around Puerto Princesa.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do you deliver around Puerto Princesa?
A: For now, we offer pickup and limited delivery within Brgy. Maunlad. We plan to expand delivery to nearby barangays soon.

A: Yes! Just text or call us to reserve. Pick-up is available same day.
Q: Do you accept GCash?
A: Yes! We accept GCash, Maya, and bank transfers. We also accept cash and card payments in-store.
Q: Are your products imported or local?
A: We sell both. We prioritize local Filipino-made brands, but also carry trusted imported items.
